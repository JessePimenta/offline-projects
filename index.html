Offline Projects is a new media experiential series, showcasing progressive underground talent in untraditional spaces.

Every OP experience follows a simple formula: forward-thinking musician + new media artist + unique space. Our vision is to bridge the gap between art and nightlife using popup site-specific experiences. Each iteration is a distinctive onetime event – never repeating, always innovating.

Online Projects is an experiential creative agency.

We partner with select clients to create innovative experiences with the spirit of Offline Projects. Using the same OP formula, we bring to life groundbreaking experiences in a full 360 degree process – from creative to production to amplification.

OPX is our weekly newsletter, your plug into OP and what’s happening under the radar in music & art with exclusive interviews and content all in a < 5 minute read.
